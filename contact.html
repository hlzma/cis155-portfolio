Contact Us

We are a family operated mobile food vendor from the Mariana Islands serving Chamorro food in the Pacific Northwest. We have officially transitioned to "Marianas Pacific Foods" after operating for a year and a half under the name — "Amazil Chamorro Chow". You can find us for some flavorful BBQ plates at the Kitsap County's Annual Pacific Islander Festival every August.
